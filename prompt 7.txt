## ⚠️ Common Issues & Fixes:

**Image Upload Error:**
> "Fix Firebase Storage image upload.
Make sure firebase_storage is initialized
and storage rules allow authenticated users
to upload to users/[uid]/profile/"

**Chart Not Showing:**
> "Fix fl_chart BarChart implementation.
Use BarChartGroupData with proper
BarChartRodData list and axis titles"

**WebView Error:**
> "Add webview_flutter package to pubspec.yaml
and fix legal screen WebView implementation"

**Navigation Error:**
> "Fix named routes. Make sure all route
names match exactly and onGenerateRoute
handles arguments properly"
